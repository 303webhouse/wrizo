## The spec is three device verdicts

M2 shipped the rhizome GREEN and Nick's eye ruled on it: **too dark;
too confined; he wants it roaming the whole ground, filled by essay
length.** M3 is those three verdicts made real, and nothing else. The
M2 review is the foundation document — its rulings (framed-only
mounting, the paper-bottom-center substitution, token discipline, the
StrictMode ref pattern, the boundary math) all stand except where a
verdict explicitly supersedes.

## S1 — the ink warmed (verdict: too dark)

`--rhizome-ink` moves from `#4A3A28` (~1.5:1 against the `#1F1A16`
ground — the review's own Q2 warning, now confirmed by Nick's eye) to
the ruled starting value **`#7A6242`** (~2.9:1 — visible ambient
presence, still far below the paper's voice). One token, the seam M2
cut for exactly this purpose. Pinned as a bounded delta (the 10px-rise
pattern): Nick's device eye at the sitting confirms or nudges the final
value. Nothing orange at rest, unchanged; the ember stays evental and
untouched.

## S2 — the roam (verdict: too confined)

The field's canvas becomes the WHOLE desk ground — the full framed-desk
background layer, edge to edge, z-order under the paper exactly as
today. Origins multiply: seeded origin points distributed across the
ground (the paper's bottom-center remains one of them — continuity with
every ground grown so far), growth wandering the entire territory. **The
paper-avoidance law is unmoved and must be re-proven at the new scale:**
`segmentTouchesRect` remains the wall; the 40-seed stress sweep re-runs
against full-ground geometry, and the number that matters is still
zero paper violations. Growth may surround the page; it may never touch
it.

## S3 — filled by essay length (verdict: the driver)

Coverage is driven by the entry's own total word count through a
saturating curve: visually full ground at **≈2,500 words** — an essay —
and stable thereafter (no unbounded segment growth past saturation; the
ground fills, then IS full). The session's progress events keep driving
the ember flare exactly as M2 built it — the incentive coupling is
untouched. The anti-gamification frame binds absolutely: no numbers, no
percentages, no counts, no meters anywhere — the rhizome testifies to
the writing's existence; it never measures it. Ruled by Fable, vetoable
at the sitting: if Nick's eye wants the fill slower or faster, the
saturation constant is one number.

## S4 — determinism and cost

Seeded by entry id — the same page grows the same ground across
reloads, per M2's engine discipline (the revisit defect's
shape-normalization stands until its own ledger item's fix). Growth
ticks ride the existing engine cadence — no per-keystroke re-layout, no
new timers. Reduced-motion: M2's branch extended to the new scale —
segments appear instantly, no strobe, the ember cross-fade unchanged.

## Definition of done

Nick opens a page he has truly written, and the ground beneath it is
alive to the edges — quiet, warm, unmistakably there, never once
touching his words. An essay's worth of writing has filled the

entire thesis, drawn instead of told.
