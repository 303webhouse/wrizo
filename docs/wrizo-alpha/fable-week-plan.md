## The reframe that governs everything

With under a week of Fable access, the scarce resource is not code — CC
persists and builds at full speed indefinitely. The scarce resource is
**Fable-tier decisions committed to disk**: canons, decision-complete
briefs, recorded priors, and the review discipline itself. Every hour spent
where CC could substitute is an hour wasted; every judgment left in Fable's
head instead of a doc is a judgment lost on handoff day. The week
therefore optimizes for one thing: **that the studio runs at full quality
after the handoff.** This is the house law — anything that must outlive a
session goes on disk — applied to the largest session boundary there is.
