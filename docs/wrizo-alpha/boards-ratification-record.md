**Disposition (Fable, accepted by Nick):** the concern removed the
migration-safety leg of R2's reasoning; the dress (derived
membership / authored arrangement) was SUSTAINED on merits alone —

provenance becomes a board-scan, and dragging a card could change

truth layer under one universal arrangement layer IS the
strong-bones architecture. **The waiver's three concrete effects,
recorded:** (1) no legacy-compat shims anywhere in the B-arc;
(2) the vestigial `shelved` flag RETIRES in B2 rather than being
dressed; (3) dev data is disposable if structure fights. The true
collapse remains a separate, schema-flagged decision nobody has
made.

"Sort of. To clarify: I still want a user to be walked through a

advance; ends by dropping the writer onto a Board with plan cards
laid out; the first card carries a subtle "Start Here" that
vanishes once ANY card is completed. **Anti-solicitation reading,
